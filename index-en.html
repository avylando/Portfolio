Discipline

I create websites and landings based on your layout. Before starting work we will hold a brief. Based on the brief I make a technical task, which describe all the details of the work ahead. We discuss technical task, agree on the price and terms of work. I create the project in strict accordance with the technical task. If you want to make changes to it, we will discuss them further. If you already have a technical task, I'm ready to work with it.

Quality

I apply modern technologies in my work. Optimize website for all popular browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Opera, Yandex, Edge, IE11 +), for different devices ("adaptive / responsive" layout). I follow the principles of "progressive enhancement" , "pixel perfect", and accessibility for users with disabilities. I'm doing the basic SEO-optimization and integration with "Wordpress".

If you need consultation or assistance, you can contact me in any convenient way. We can agree on a permanent maintenance and support of the site, if you need it. If you have any ideas and suggestions about cooperation, I am always ready to consider them.
